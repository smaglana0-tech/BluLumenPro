Is BluLumen Pro safe to use?
Yes — it's completely drug-free and non-invasive. No medications are involved, and the device only activates when worn correctly to protect your eyes from direct light.
How quickly will I feel relief?
Many users report noticeable relief within minutes of their first session. Results may vary depending on your condition and how often you use the device.
What conditions can it help with?
BluLumen Pro is designed for arthritis, plantar fasciitis, carpal tunnel syndrome, tendon and ligament recovery, and general joint soreness and inflammation.
Are there any side effects?
No known side effects have been reported. Unlike medications or invasive procedures, BluLumen Pro works naturally with your body's own healing processes.
How long should I use it each session?
A typical session lasts about 20 minutes. It can be used multiple times a day if needed. Most users find once or twice daily gives the best results.
